FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How many lessons will I need?

Most learners need 30-45 hours. Our structured approach typically gets students test-ready in 35-40 hours. We assess progress every 10 lessons.

Do you pick up from home or work?

Yes — we pick up from home, work, or any convenient location within our service area (Central London, North London, and surrounding areas).

Is the test fee included?

Test fees are paid directly to the DVSA (Theory: £23, Practical: £62). Our prices cover tuition and car hire options. We can help you book your test.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Yes — contact us and we'll arrange a digital voucher.

What areas do you cover?

We serve Central London, North London, and surrounding areas including Islington, Camden, Westminster, and parts of Hackney and Haringey.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for bundles and intensive courses. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.
